A.1 PRIMARY TEXTS

Aeschylus. Aeschylus, with an English translation by Herbert Weir Smyth, Ph.D. 2 vols. Cambridge, Mass.: Harvard University Press; London: William Heinemann, 1926. (Modernized by Cynthia Bannon, Graeme Bird, Gregory Crane, James Erdmann, Annette Giesecke, and Nancy Sultan. Reviewed by John Gibert.)

Apollodorus. Apollodorus, The Library, with an English Translation by Sir James George Frazer, F.B.A., F.R.S. 2 vols. Vols. 1, 2, Books 1-3. Cambridge, Mass.: Harvard University Press; London: William Heinemann, 1921. (Includes Frazer's notes and index.)

Herodotus. Herodotus, with an English Translation by A. D. Godley. 4 vols. Cambridge, Mass.: Harvard University Press; London: William Heinemann, 1926. (Modernized by Annette Giesecke, Steven Ott, and Lynn Sawlivich. Reviewed by John Marincola. Includes index.)

Homer. Homeri Opera in Five Volumes. Vols. 1, 2: Iliad. Edited by David B. Monro and Thomas W. Allen. Oxford: Oxford University Press, 1920.

Homer. The Iliad, with an English Translation by A. T. Murray, Ph.D. 2 vols. Cambridge, Mass.: Harvard University Press; London: William Heinemann, 1924. (English text only.)

Homer. The Odyssey, with an English Translation by A. T. Murray, Ph.D. 2 vols. Cambridge, Mass.: Harvard University Press; London: William Heinemann, 1924.

Pausanias. Pausaniae Graeciae Descriptio. 3 vols. Edited by F. Spiro. Leipzig: Teubner, 1903.

Pausanias. Pausanias, Description of Greece, with an English Translation by W. H. S. Jones, Litt.D. 4 vols. Cambridge, Mass.: Harvard University Press; London: William Heinemann, 1918. (English text only.)

Pindar. Odes. Translated by Diane Svarlien, 1990. (Unpublished.)

Pindar. The Odes of Pindar, including the Principal Fragments, with an Introduction and an English Translation by Sir John Sandys, Litt.D., F.B.A. Cambridge, Mass.: Harvard University Press; London: William Heinemann, 1937. (Greek only.)

Plutarch. Plutarch's Lives, with an English Translation by Bernadotte Perrin. 11 vols. Cambridge, Mass.: Harvard University Press; London: William Heinemann, 1916. (Lives of Alcibiades, Aristides, Cimon, Lysander, Miltiades, Nicias, Pericles, Solon, Themistocles, and Theseus.)

Sophocles. Edited with introduction and notes by Sir Richard Jebb. Cambridge: Cambridge University Press, 1887-1898. (Modernized by Roger Ceragioli, Pierre Habel, Lynn Sawlivich, and Alex Sens. Reviewed by John Gibert. )

Thucydides. Historiae in Two Volumes. Edited by Henry Stuart Jones and Johannes Enoch Powell. Oxford: Oxford University Press, 1942.

Thucydides. The Peloponnesian War. Translated by Richard Crawley. London: J. M. Dent; New York: E. P. Dutton, 1910.
